Hi, you must be new around here. I'm a WeeNAS system. I'm the unobtrusive little box that stores files for people. Before we start working together, I'd like you to tell me a secret.

You see, I don't really do the whole password thing, so a secret is how I know it's you. A secret can be almost anything, but should be something only you and I know.

You could tell me a secret like, "Rocky Road is absolutely my most favorite ice cream flavor in the whole entire world." Or it could be something shorter, like "All my socks have holes." Be creative, but make sure you can remember it.

When you find yourself needing help with something, like pesky system passwords you might have forgotten, just come back here and tell me your secret. I'll know it's you and then I can help you reset those passwords. I'll help you set your initial passwords, too.
